Who We Are

From a single workshop
to Malaysia's trusted
fabrication partner.

Ekarbina was founded in 1988 with a single premise: that signage should be treated as architecture — permanent, precise, and worthy of the brand it carries. What began as a small workshop in Kuala Lumpur has grown into one of Malaysia's most respected signage fabrication specialists.

Over more than three decades, we have completed over 800 projects spanning corporate towers, luxury retail, hospitality, healthcare, and public infrastructure. Our work spans every material — from energy-efficient LED modules to hand-finished brass letterforms — all produced in-house.

We do not subcontract fabrication. Every sign that leaves our workshop was designed, built, and quality-checked by our own team. That is the Ekarbina standard, and it has not changed since 1988.

Our History

Three decades,
one standard.

From hand-painted fascias to precision LED arrays — our craft has evolved, but our commitment to quality has remained unchanged since the day we opened.

  1. 1988

    Founded in Kuala Lumpur

    Ekarbina opens its first workshop, specialising in hand-crafted fascia and flat-cut lettering for local retail clients.

  2. Mid-1990s

    Expanding into Illuminated Signage

    Adoption of neon and early fluorescent box signage, serving growing commercial districts across the Klang Valley.

  3. Early 2000s

    LED & Large-Format Fabrication

    Investment in CNC routing and early LED technology. First pylon structures completed for anchor retail tenants.

  4. 2010s

    Premium Metal & Architectural Work

    Growth into stainless steel and brass dimensional lettering for luxury hospitality and corporate headquarters.

  5. Today

    800+ Projects, Still Growing

    A full-service signage studio — design through installation — trusted by Malaysia's leading brands.

How We Work

The principles behind
every project.

01

Precision above all

Tolerances measured in millimetres. Every cut, weld, and finish is held to the same exacting standard — regardless of project size.

02

In-house, end-to-end

We never subcontract fabrication. Design, production, and installation are all performed by our own team — no handoffs, no loss of quality.

03

Materials that endure

We specify only materials built to outlast the warranty. Because a sign that fades or corrodes within five years reflects on your brand, not ours.

Our People

Founder

Wong Fook Kong

Founder & Master Craftsman

Co-Founder

Jennifer Lee

Co-Founder & Operations Director

In 1988, Fook Kong started with a pair of skilled hands, a modest workshop in Salak South, and a conviction that every business — no matter how small — deserves a sign worthy of its name.

Jennifer came into his life and into the business not long after. She brought order, warmth, and an eye for what clients truly needed. He shaped metal and light into signs that lasted decades. She made sure those signs reached the people who needed them most. Together, they built something neither could have built alone.

More than three decades on, they are still here — in the same workshop, holding the same standard. Not because it is easy. But because when you build something with the person you love, you build it to last.

"We do not just make signs. We make things that last."

— Wong Fook Kong, Founder
